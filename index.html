What is it?

Welcome to Machine Learning Village! Our goal is to encourage you to explore ML topics which are key to Adobe.

We have 7 hands-on labs that will walk you through 4 important topics. Please do not feel the need to read every word, the point instead is to get the feel of the parts of each lab that are most interesting to you. Labs will indicate which sections are safe to skip.

Below is a list of labs with some meta information... ok, enough said, what would you like to learn?

To help everyone enjoy ML Village during Tech Summit please do not go back to platform to finish the lab this week. We have detailed instructions of how to run this on your own machine in a way that keeps resources available for all.

How does it work?

  • Check in with volunteer or registration
  • Pick a lab
  • Explore the content
  • Ask questions of volunteers... or your neighbours
  • (SF only) Check in with a volunteer or registration again
  • Don't forget to grab our url and visit us later
    *All the labs can be run on your own machine after Tech Summit
